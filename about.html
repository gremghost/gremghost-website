Shocking, Isn't it?

This is my very first album, made in 2017. I used to only use OpenMPT
before properly being able to use / understand FL Studio or any DAW in general.
My main goal was to make an album. I always wanted to but had never been able to.
And it was a fun time for me. I was honestly struggling to get good at finishing the
songs I came up with but this album and really the timeframe as a whole was when
I finished my first song (Shock Confusion) and then was able to make enough
to make this release. Some of the songs nowadays I'm not too proud of but some
of these are still favorites of mine to listen to. It's also almost ten years old which is
absolutely wild.

2. Worth It
